About BLM

"Black Lives Matter" is a movement founded in 2013 after Trayvon Martin was shot and killed unarmed by George Zimmerman, who was charged with murder, but acquitted after claiming that it was an act of self defense, even though Trayvon Martin was unarmed and had no intentions of hurting George Zimmmerman. Black Lives Matter affirms the lives of lgbtq, trans, disabled, undocumented black people, and all black people in general. The mission or goal of the Black Lives Matter movement is to end white supremacy and end police brutality and killings towards innocent people of color.

You can read more about the movement here.

Also, watch this short video about Black Lives Matter.

In short...

  • BLM is a movement founded in 2013
  • BLM was created after Trayvon Martin's killer was acquitted from a trial where he was charged with murder.
  • BLM affirms the lives of all black people
  • The goal of BLM is to end police brutality towards innocent black people and end white supremacy

The Problem: Police Brutality

Police Brutality is an issue that has been in the world for decades. Police Brutality is defined as a civil rights violation in whcih officers use excessive, unnecessary, brutal, and sometimes lethal force against a person. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Bullying
  • Property Damage
  • Physical or Mental Injury
  • Physical or Verbal Harassment
  • Death
